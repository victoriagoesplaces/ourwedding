Victoria + Jason Meet

Victoria and Jason met in 2015 during their Disney College Program. They were best friends from day one, taking trips to Earl of Sandwich together and bonding over a mutal love of travel.

June 2016

Jason Asks Victoria Out

In 2016, Jason asked Victoria out on a date to which she said maybe.

Jason Asks

visit Nashville
